The Search for Enlightenment

Cosmic Enthusiasm is a page dedicated to those that would prefer to know further about the vast and potentially limitless heavens above us. We are a group of people working together to create a community of individuals who can come together in their pursuit of knowledge. Kindly take note we are currently not a business or an educational institute. We are currently limited to this page and our followers but we might expand in the future.
